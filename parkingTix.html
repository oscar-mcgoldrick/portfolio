Parking Tix

This was our final group project during the Dev Academy bootcamp, where our team of 5 spent just under a week developing an automatic parking ticket disputing application.

A user can sign-up & sign in with their Gmail account, and create & manage ongoing disputes. I was responsible for all Gmail API integration, and thoroughly enjoyed the challenge of understanding the google packages.
